There are a number of great resources out here for us here in Washington State. Probably one of the best resources is WA Law Help their page on Family Law is particularly useful. For the most up to date forms the best place is of course the source:Courts.wa.gov/forms all of the up to date forms are here for download in both Microsoft Word and Adobe PDF forms.

The first piece of advice that I have when it comes to court is this: be the person that files first. Even if you are not the person who is primarily pushing for dissolution of a marriage or relationship, File first. You are at a serious disadvantage if the other party files before you do. What next? at a minimum 2 forms.
FL Divorce 200 which is a formal summons about any of 5 actions to break a partnership or marriage. This gives the other party 20 days from the date paperwork is served to them to respond in writing. 60 days if out of state. BOTH to the person who started this action "Petitioner" and file with the court. and FL divorce 201 which is the "meat and potatoes" of the information about the terms of the end of your marriage or domestic partnership.
For ease of serving paperwork, I recommend also filing All Civil 006 which is an Email Service Agreement.
There are a lot of other things that need to be filed with the court in combination with Divorce 201.
FL 135 Declaration which is for general statements made that are relavent to the case. FL All Family 131 which is a financial declaration. It is probably a good idea to also use FL011 Sealed Financial Documents cover sheet to protect your personal financial information.
Everything filed without a cover sheet is a public record that can be requested by anyone. PROTECT YOUR PRIVACY!
Other cover sheets that maybe useful are: FL013 Sealed Confidential Report and FL012 Health Care Records
The most difficult part of divorce or ending a domestic partnership is when there are children involved. There are a number of different things that need to be filed. The first and most important is FL140 a parenting plan this can be modified at a later date with FL Modify 601. If the divorce is finalized, you also need to file FL600. Along with the financial declaration, a child support worksheet needs to be filled out and submitted. This is why it is important to submit as much financial documentation as possible. not just for the division of assets, but to ensure that there is substantiating evidence of what portion is to be paid in case of any dispute that may arise. It is important to note that this ammount can be adjusted via Form FL501. This needs to be filed with Form FL500.
