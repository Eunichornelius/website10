Navigating Familiy Law in Kitsap County
without an Attorney

Going to court, filing legal paperwork are already daunting enough tasks without the stress that usually precedes and persists going through this process.

I am not an Attorney nor a Paralegal, this information is just presented as is from personal experience and research I've done to try and navigate this process. I'm just a grumpy man with a cat.
Please do not take anything from this page as legal advice.

There are a number of free resources out there that can be used to prepare legal documents. I have links on the Resources page as well as an explaination of what each site brings to the table. There is some ridiculous legal speak out there which seems like it is there just to confuse you. I've tried to unpack some of this in the Jargon page.
There is no money for an Attorney to make representing someone in family law unless they see a way to take from the other party in the marriage and as such, you will probably not find a Lawyer as the bread winner in a family unless you have around 10 thousand dollars to put down as a retainer.
Surprise! If you are in this situation, you will not qualify for representation "pro bono" or a public defender.
