Navigating Familiy Law in Kitsap County without an Attorney

Going to court, filing legal paperwork are already daunting enough tasks without the stress that usually precedes and persists going through this process. I've tried to compile the basic information needed to disolve a marriage or domestic partnership here.

I am not an Attorney nor a Paralegal, this information is just presented as is from personal experience and research I've done to try and navigate this process. I'm just a grumpy man with a cat.
Please do not take anything from this page as legal advice.

There are a number of free resources out there that can be used to prepare legal documents. I have links on the Resources page as well as an explaination of what each site brings to the table. There is some ridiculous legal speak out there which seems like it is there just to confuse you. I've tried to unpack some of this in the Jargon page.
You can save yourself a lot of money with a little bit of leg work and a basic understanding of court rules and procedings. An exhaustive list of court rules in Kitsap County is available.
For those that qualify Kitsap Legal Aid Services is a good resource.
While there is an E-File System in Kitsap Country, you must file bench copies in person so you won't save a trip to the court house.
